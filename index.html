    HOW TO USE WORKSTATION

    1. Note down all your tasks

    2. Start the Pomodoro timer

    3. After one Pomodoro session, have a short break

    4. After three Pomodoro sessions, have a long break

    5. During your work flow, if you have any distracting thoughts, jot them down in the notes area
